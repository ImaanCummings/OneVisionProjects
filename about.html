ABOUT US. OUR STORY.

One Vision Keys was founded by Mr Mogamad Cummings 18 years ago in Claremont. After 10 successful years, he ventured into the building trade. When COVID-19 hit and everything went quiet, his passion and purpose called him back to locksmithing. Today we are proudly based in Surrey Estate and continue to offer mobile locksmith services across Cape Town.
